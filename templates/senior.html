Point cutting

Point cutting is used to remove bulk from the hairâ€™s ends, allowing layers or graduation built into the haircut to blend together more seamlessly.

Slice cutting

Slice cutting is a technique used on a finished haircut. This removes weight, length and tapers the ends of the hair. It allows very steep graduation to be cut into long hair.

Barbering

The scissor over comb technique is one of the most frequently used techniques for barbers. It allows the barber to cut close to the head, following the hairline for precise results.

Thinning & Texturising

To thin the hair is simply to remove bulk or thickness from the hair but to retain the length of the hair during cutting.
